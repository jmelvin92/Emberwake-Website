Short Biography

Emberwake is a post-hardcore/metalcore band known for their explosive blend of crushing breakdowns, atmospheric passages, and emotionally charged lyrics. Their 2024 album "Anhedonia" explores themes of emotional numbness and existential struggle, establishing them as a powerful voice in the modern metal scene.

With their intense live performances and dedication to authentic expression, Emberwake has quickly built a devoted following in the underground metal community. The band continues to push boundaries while staying true to their heavy roots.
